Delete Account & App Data

RunnSeegaa respects your privacy. You can request deletion of your account and associated app data at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email from your registered email address
  2. Mention "Delete My Account & Data" in the subject
  3. Send to: support@runnseegaa.com

Data that will be deleted

  • User account information
  • Email address
  • Profile data associated with the account

Retention

Data will be permanently deleted within 7 working days after verification.

If you have any questions, contact us at support@runnseegaa.com
